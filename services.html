Acrylic Extensions

From €80

⏱ 90 minutes

Acrylic extensions remain the gold standard for nail length and structure. We sculpt each nail using a liquid-and-powder system applied either over nail tips or directly onto the natural nail using forms. The result is a strong, durable extension that can be shaped to any silhouette — square, squoval, almond, coffin, or stiletto. Once cured, the surface is finished to your preference: natural, gel colour, chrome, or full nail art. Infills recommended every 3 weeks.

  • Full set or infills available
  • Tip or form sculpting
  • All shapes and lengths
  • Odour-reduced, low-dust formula

Soft Gel Extensions

From €70

⏱ 75 minutes

For those who want length without the rigidity of acrylics, soft gel extensions offer a more natural, flexible alternative. Applied using pre-shaped gel capsules or builder gel with forms, these extensions feel closer to natural nails and are gentler on the nail bed during removal. They're an excellent choice for clients transitioning from acrylics or those with more active lifestyles. Finished with gel polish, chrome, or art.
