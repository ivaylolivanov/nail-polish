Our Founding

A Passion Project
Grown Into an Atelier

Maison Vernì was founded in 2016 by Vivienne Laurent, a trained beauty therapist with a background in fine arts. After years working in high-end Parisian salons, Vivienne grew tired of the rushed, assembly-line approach that had become the norm. She wanted to build something different — a place where craft came first.

The name comes from the French word for nail polish, vernis, and the Italian suffix suggesting something lovingly made. It's a small nod to the dual heritage that shapes how we work: French precision and Italian warmth.

Today, Maison Vernì has grown from a single-chair studio into a full atelier with a team of seven specialists. Every one of them is handpicked not just for technical skill, but for patience, artistry, and genuine love of the work.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01

Craft Over Speed

We never rush. We book adequate time for every service because precision requires patience. You'll never feel hurried out of your chair.

02

Clean Beauty

All products we use are cruelty-free and formulated without the toxic trio: toluene, formaldehyde, and DBP. We believe beautiful nails shouldn't come at a cost to your health.

03

Genuine Consultation

We ask questions. We listen. A good nail appointment begins well before the first stroke of polish — it begins with understanding what you actually want.

04

Constant Learning

Our team trains every quarter in new techniques, tools, and trends. We invest in our craft so you always receive work that reflects the current best.
